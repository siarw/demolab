About

Innovation through Space

At NL PhiLab, we nurture pioneering research into disruptive technologies with strong commercial potential. As part of the European Space Agency's PhiLabNET, we forge the connections needed to propel the future of space innovation and enhance Europe's global competitiveness.

Innovation through Collaboration

We are driven to explore how collaboration between the research sector and the market sector can yield groundbreaking innovations, and sustainable business strategies.

Themes

We focus on Earth Observation, Position, Navigation and Timing, and Laser Satellite Communication, but we will support any demonstrably good project or idea.

Where You Come In

Apply to participate in collaborative research projects and develop innovative solutions. You will be supported along the way and have the chance to develop new partnerships in the space sector.
